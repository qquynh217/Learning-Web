About company & products

MADE WITH LOVE IN CALIFORNIA

Lèvres was born, raised and made with love in the City of Angels. Founded in 2014 by Seed Beauty®, we pride ourselves on being wallet friendly and bunny approved. We test our products in the nicest way possible, leaving fur babies to be fur babies and experimenting on people instead (the people at Lèvres HQ to be exact!).

With the right color of lipstick, you can change your entire appearance. The shade of your lipstick has the power to reflect your true personality as well as adding aesthetic value to your appearance.

No girl can never be ready for any event without a lipstick any day. As a matter of fact, most women and some men carry various shades of lipsticks with them when they travel. Lipstick is really magical. It holds more than a waxy bit of color - it holds the promise of a brilliant smile, a brilliant day, both literally and figuratively.
